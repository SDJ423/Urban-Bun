Catch Us on the Road

As a traveling food truck, we don't have a fixed address. Instead, we bring our delicious hot dogs to various locations across the city and beyond! Our truck is always on the move, serving up mouthwatering creations wherever we go. We travel to different cities and states, so no matter where you are, there's a chance we could be nearby.

How to Find Us

To stay updated on our location, follow us on social media and sign up for our newsletter! We post our schedule regularly, so you'll know exactly where we'll be next. Whether we're at a local event, a food truck rally, or just cruising the city streets, you can always find us serving up hot dogs with a side of fun!
